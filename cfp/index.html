Call For Proposals, Due March 15, 2017

We are looking for 30 minute long talks on both technical and community topics related to User Experience. This means anything from web, mobile, and back end systems that improve UX on the web to talks about research, accessibility, team communication, and increasing inclusivity and diversity in tech. If you need inspiration, check out talks from 2016 and 2015.

Submit your talk proposals here. Submissions will close on March 15th, 2017 at 11:59:59 pm Eastern.

Your proposals will be anonymized in the first round of judging and will be selected by a panel of volunteers. Please refrain from using identifying information in your titles, abstracts, and notes to the panel. Please review our diversity statement and code of conduct to ensure the content of your talk is in line with our community guidelines.

All speakers will receive a free ticket to the conference and travel assistance, if needed. Please reach out to the UX Burlington team if you have any questions: {{site.email}}
